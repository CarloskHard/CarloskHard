## 👨‍💻 About me

I've been programming for **over 7 years**. My journey in tech is a bit unusual: my professional career started as an **electronics technician** (aerospace electronics and PCB design). While it was rewarding, I had already spent hours programming since I was 17, driven by my love for video game design. After years of coding as a hobby, I realized it was my true passion and earned degrees in **Web Application Development (DAW)** and **Multiplatform Application Development (DAM)**. I started by developing remote control systems for iOS devices, and today I specialize in **Fullstack Web Development (ERPs and CRMs)** and **Multiplatform Apps**.

*   🏛️ **My coding philosophy:** I'm obsessed with **clean architectures**. Writing spaghetti code and leaving behind messy projects is definitely not my vibe. I absolutely enjoy studying design patterns, **SOLID** principles, and **Clean Architecture**; creating code that not only works but is scalable, maintainable, and beautiful under the hood.
*   🎨 **In the Frontend:** I love creating interfaces that are pleasant, intuitive, and, why not, a little bit fun.
*   📝 **Documentation:** It might sound like a joke, but I *genuinely* enjoy documenting processes and projects technically and in detail.

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🍽️ [Platorama](#) *(In development - 2025)*
**The project:** My most ambitious personal project. A gastronomic social network that creates a space for users to manage their favorite spots and seek recommendations from friends.
**Tech:** Developed under a *Clean Architecture* to recycle business logic and presentation across platforms.
*   **Frontend/App:** Android Studio + Compose Multiplatform + Kotlin.
*   **Backend/Auth:** Firebase (NoSQL and authentication).
*   **Local DB:** SQLite with Room as an abstraction layer.

### 🎨[Web Portfolio & E-Commerce (La Bicha Tattoo)](https://labichatattoo.es/) *(2025)*
**The project:** Freelance development of a complete platform for a tattoo artist, including an appointment system, an SEO-optimized portfolio, and an online store.
**Tech:** WordPress + WooCommerce.
*   *Note:* A great example showing that for simple and rapid deployment projects (2 days), true power lies in knowing how to leverage established tools (CMS) to deliver the best solution to the client without reinventing the wheel.
